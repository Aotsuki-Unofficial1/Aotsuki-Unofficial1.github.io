KALIX.exe — Hardware Audit
How It Works

Three phases. Under a second.

Phase 01

Component Enumeration

Direct hardware enumeration APIs — bypassing OS abstraction. CPU topology, PCI hierarchy, memory channels, firmware strings extracted at raw device level. Serial cross-reference detects cloning instantly.

Phase 02

Thermal Intelligence

Direct sensor-bus polling at 100ms intervals across CPU and GPU. Junction temperature, TDP headroom, clock frequency, voltage state. Throttle events captured with full system snapshots.

Phase 03

Integrity Verification

SHA-256 manifest cryptographically binding the audit to exact hardware state at time of execution. Deterministic, reproducible, and immutable across identical hardware states.
